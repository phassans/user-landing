Real-Time Deals at Any Hour

Fast and easy

You’ll immediately find real-time discounts on countless food and drinks when you open the app. Whether 50% off lunches, $1 tacos, or BOGO drink specials, they’ll always be at your fingertips.

No coupons

We hate coupons as much as you do. That’s why HungryHour is as coupon-less as it gets. No vouchers, no scanning, no hassle. All you need to do is show up, order, and enjoy!

Discounts you’ll never miss

How It Works

HungryHour’s mission is to allow everyone to experience new and tasty eats without breaking the bank. We do this by partnering with hundreds of local restaurants that want to share their food with you in the easiest way possible--through great discounts. You get to save money while helping these small businesses grow and succeed.
